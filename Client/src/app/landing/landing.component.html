MetalUp.Express is an online 'Integrated Learning Environment' for programming, in several different programming languages, designed specifically for use within schools, collegues, and universities.

Using the system

To use MetalUp.Express you will need to be registered as a Student or a Teacher (see below). If you are already registered, then please Log In to use the system. (If you are not already registered then just logging in still won't get you access to any of content.) Clicking the Log In button will generate a pop-up managed by the 'Auth0' service. This will allow you to log in using your Google, GitHub, or othe recognised credentials, but once you have registered please always log in using the same credentials, as these will be associated with your User profile.

How to register

If you are a Student, you need to have an invitation (by email) from a teacher already registered on MetalUp.Express. Just follow the link in the email that the teacher sends you.

If you are a Teacher, and would like to be able to use MetalUp.Express with your students please email Richard Pawson as rpawson@metalup.org. MetalUp.Express is completely free of charge for use in schools, colleages, and universities. Please provide details of your educational institution, your role within in. Important: please provide clear evidence that you are a teacher, lecturer or equivalent, at your institution, ideally a link showing your name and role on the institutions website. Please also ensure that you are sending the email from an email address associated with your institution.Please note that this information is manually reviewed, and while we typically respond the same day, it can take a few days depending upon our availability.

If your application is successful you will be emailed with instructions on how to explore the projects available on MetalpUp.Express, invite students and/or other teacher colleagues to participate, assign projects to students, track their progress, and administer your own organisational and student data.

Privacy policy

MetalUp.Express stores as little personal information as possible to allow the system to function:

  1. If you are a Teacher, we hold your name, institution details, and your email address. We may be in contact with you concerning the use of MetalUp.Express, but your details will not be used for marketing purposes, nor passed on to any third party. Your log-in identity is stored only in encrypted form.
  2. If you are a Student then it is up to your Teacher(s) to determine how you are identified: for example by first name only, initials, and/or student number. MetalUp.Express does not require specific identity details for students. Your log-in identity is stored only in encrypted form. We do not require, or store, email addresses for students.
