Explains what Express is and does

Explains that you need to be registered either as a Teacher or as a Student. If you already are, then please log in here.; if you are not already registered and you log in you won't be able to access any of the system's functionality.

Clicking the login button will generate a pop-up managed by the 'Auth0 service.

This will allow you to login using your Google, or other recognised account.

How to register

If you are a Student, you need to have an invitation (by email) from a teacher already registered on Express. Just follow the link in the email the teacher sends you.

If you are a Teacher, and would like to be able to use MetalUp with your students please complete and submit this form. There is no charge for this, but you will be asked to submit evidence of your status as a teacher at a school, college, or university. Please note that this information is manually reviewed and while we typically respond the same day, it can take a few days depending upon our availability. If your application is successful you will be emailed with instructions on how to explore the projects available on MetalpUp.Express, invite students and/or other teacher colleagues to participate, assign projects to students, track their progress, and administer your own organisational and student data.
